How to Use

  1. Click the button in the bottom-right corner to cycle modes
  2. Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+O (Cmd+Shift+O on Mac)
  3. Three modes: Off → Normal (divs only) → Enhanced (all elements)

The tool remembers your preference using localStorage.
